Salve Mundi

The study association of Fontys HBO-ICT

the media
committee

I am studying ICT at Fontys Hogescholen in Eindhoven. To maintain contact with fellow students and to build up a life in the city, I am a member of the Fontys ICT study association called Salve Mundi (Latin for Hello World). I have been volunteering within the association since June 2021. After being a member of the media committee for six months, I moved up to the position of committee leader in January 2022.

The media committee is the creative core of Salve Mundi. From cool designs on intro shirts and hoodies, to promotional materials and photography at parties and events. In short, the media committee creates the appearance of Salve Mundi.

During my time on the media committee, I created numerous posters for parties and events. I have taken and edited photos at events. I had three sales drops of merchandise that I designed myself. I redesigned the Salve Mundi website. And currently, I manage the Instagram and create posts for this as well. Besides all these design related tasks, I also had to do a lot of administrative work. Think of typing for schedules or calculating costs. The team of media committee members supports me in all these tasks.

merch drop

hoodie for
active members

There are many volunteers working within Salve Mundi. To thank all these people for their efforts, the board wanted to give free hoodies to every active member. We as the media committee were given the task of designing these.

Together with another committee member, I took on this task. We created mood boards of what we had in mind, and continuously communicated with the board to see if we were on the same page. After getting approval for each step, I finally started designing. Here you can see the end result combined with some images of the process.

posters

for events
and parties

Both as a committee member and committee leader, I have put up an enormous amount of posters for parties and events within Salve Mundi. I always made these in Adobe Photoshop. It is important to think about what the client wants, and to keep in close contact. Usually I receive a request with the information that needs to be on the poster. Sometimes I also get some examples with it.

Then I do my best to make a poster according to the request. Occasionally I make several posters, and then I let the client choose which one they like best. I make every poster square shaped so that it can be easily posted on Salve Mundi's Instagram page.

merch drop

for new
members

Since my start as committee leader of the media committee in January 2022, I have brought a new concept to life. Yearly member hoodies. These are sweaters for new members of Salve Mundi. Each year gets a different color, so you can see at a glance in which year someone started studying. These hoodies will be sold at the induction. The first event new members go to. In addition to the hoodies, sweatpants and sunglasses are also sold at induction. I created the designs for all of these merchandise items.

redesigning the
website

Salve Mundi has had the same website for some time. They are struggling with some problems on the backend of the site. If they are going to have to rewrite the entire backend of the site anyway, why not the front-end as well? That's why the IT committee of Salve Mundi approached me to ask if I wanted to redesign their site. I was happy to do so!

For the redesign of the site I used Figma. During the design I took into account the corporate identity of Salve Mundi. In between I have had continuous contact with the IT committee to ask for their opinion. I have always neatly processed the feedback they came up with. The final design is now finished. The only thing left to do is to make it reality.

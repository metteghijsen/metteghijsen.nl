Fontys Engineering

My work as a visual designer for Fontys Engineering.

talking about
my side job

As a side job, during my first and second year I worked at Fontys Centre of Expertise, a department that falls within Fontys Engineering. Every semester Fontys organizes a symposium for all its students. This way students can show their work, and get in contact with large companies.

My task was to create all the web pages within the Fontys website. These webpages were used to showcase the students' projects. I also had to make posters, Photoshop templates, cards and LinkedIn posts for promotion. I also made the visual presentation for during the symposium.

All the webpages weren't made using plain HTML, CSS and Javascript. Instead they were made using a content management system. Since I worked with this system for over a year, I became proficient with the program.

for students

At the second edition of Engineering & Technology in Practice, there was not only a symposium, but also an exhibition in the school building. Students could place their school project somewhere in the building. In order to match projects to the right students, posters had to be made.

My task was to create a PSD template for these posters in the style of Fontys Engineering that the students could fill out.

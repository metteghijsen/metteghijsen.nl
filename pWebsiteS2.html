Portfolio S2

A website for displaying schoolwork from my 2nd semester.

my first
website

In the first year of my ICT & Media Design course, we had to create a portfolio website to display all our school work. Besides the content of this portfolio, we were also judged on the design and UX.

This was my first time creating a website. So you will notice that this site is not responsive at all. I also didn't spend a whole lot of time on the design, because I wanted to upload content as fast as possible. When creating my site, I mostly looked at how other students were designing theirs. That doesn't take away from the fact that I made a fine website. In fact, I passed the semester with an Outstanding thanks to this site.

Be sure to check out the site if you want an in-depth look at my work from the first year.

learning

a brief
explanation

At Fontys, we pass our semester based on how we perform per learning outcome. Therefore, it is important that you always sort your work according to each learning outcome. In my second semester, there were a total of six learning outcomes, specifically: Interactive Media, Development, Design, Research, Communication and Professional Identity.

Since these learning outcomes are the most important of the semester, I made sure to build my website around them. Before you can see my projects, you have to click on which learning outcome you want to view. You can also see this in earlier iterations of the design.

Note: Nowadays when I design a website I use Adobe XD or Figma. When I was just in the first year of my course, I didn't know anything about this yet. I was still using Photoshop then, since I already had a lot of prior knowledge about the program. Because of that, not much time was spent on designing.
